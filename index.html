About Me

Sean Ishikawa is a UCSD graduate with a Computer Engineering degree. He built his desktop during college and enjoys playing various games on it. He is a certified FileMaker developer working as a Software Engineer at Productive Computing Inc.

He got married in March of 2018 to his beautiful wife and is now taking part of the UCSD coding bootcamp to get new skills to expand his career into web development.

At Productive Computing he works with clients to get requests for customizations to their FileMaker solutions.
Some projects he has worked on include a simple word guessing game, trivia game, a Train scheduler utilizing Firebase, and a Google Maps Integration for a bar finder.
When not working, Sean enjoys playing on his PC and Vive VR headset and the occasional run. He and his wife have done the San Diego Hot Chocoloate 15k run 3 years in a row.
